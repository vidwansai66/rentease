## 1. Executive Summary
RentEase is a premium furniture and appliance rental platform designed to cater to the transient urban population in India. By offering high-quality home essentials on a flexible subscription basis, RentEase eliminates the burden of heavy capital expenditure and long-term ownership. Our mission is to democratize premium living through "Asset-Light" consumption.

---

## 2. Problem Statement & Solution
### The Problem
- **High Mobility:** India's workforce is increasingly mobile, moving across cities for better career opportunities.
- **Financial Burden:** Purchasing premium furniture requires significant upfront investment.
- **Resale Hassles:** Selling second-hand furniture is time-consuming and often results in heavy depreciation losses.
- **Logistics & Maintenance:** Moving furniture across cities is expensive and prone to damage.

### The RentEase Solution
- **Zero Down Payment:** Customers pay only the monthly rent and a nominal security deposit.
- **End-to-End Service:** Free delivery, professional installation, and lifelong maintenance are included.
- **Flexibility:** Customers can upgrade, return, or relocate their rentals for free as their lifestyle changes.
- **Smart Asset Management:** IoT-enabled appliance tracking to ensure proactive maintenance and asset longevity.
